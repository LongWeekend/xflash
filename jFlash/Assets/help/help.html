Thank you for choosing Japanese Flash.

As fellow language students, we wanted to create the best flash card program for studying words and phrases in Japanese & English.

Japanese Flash has lots of data to help you learn (180+ sets), an intelligent card selection algorithm that makes the most of your study time, and a full dictionary searchable in English, Romaji or Japanese.

Large sets are also a snap! Japanese Flash intelligently chooses 30 cards for you to work on at time. As you master cards in this selection, new cards are added. So you can methodically & effectively get through even the largest sets (e.g. 10,000 words!)

Can't read kanij yet? Don't worry! You can show readings in kana or romaji and we even remember your preference to show/hide the reading whilst you practice.

This is only version 1.0, and we have a lot planned. We hope you enjoy the Japanese Flash!

Click on the 'Study Sets' tab and choose a set by touching its name. Sets are organized into folders and are also searchable.

To search, slide your finger down the screen to reveal a search bar. Search results are updated in real time, and they are fast!

Tap on the 'Study Sets' tab and press '+' to add a set. You can add cards to the set using the Word Search. You can also add cards to a set whilst in practice mode using the 'Add To Set' button.

We believe an iPhone is a great way to practice, but not great for creating flash cards. So in version one, this functionality is limited.

You'll be pleased to hear that our roadmap includes some pretty special solutions to this problem.

back

Once a set is chosen, you can either browse cards or test yourself in practice mode. Card meanings are hidden until you touch the card area. Once revealed, you can mark a card as 'Right', 'Wrong', or 'Go Away'. Clicking 'Go Away' put the card directly into the 'Learned' category. You can also add it to other sets with the 'Add To Set' button.

The first time you get a card right, it moves from 'Studying' to 'Right 1x'. Each time you get it right, it will be promoted to a higher level.

Get it right five times and it will be marked as 'Learned'. The progress pop-up shows detailed results for the current set. Tap the progress bar to display it.

Your goal is to get each card right 5 times.
Good luck!

If you feel like some mental warm-ups before practicing, tap the 'Settings' tab and flick the 'Browse Mode' on/off switch. Now you are in browse mode, so you can move backwards & forwards through the set by swiping or tapping next/last.

Japanse Flash has well over 145,000 words separated into more than 100 sets. Word search can return over 200 results in 1-3 seconds using English, Romaji or Japanese keywords.

Search works best if you look for whole words and use the plain or infinitive form for verbs. You can enter multiple search terms at once in either language.

If your initial search returns no results. you will be allowed to perform a 'Deep Search' that usually returns more matches because it looks for words starting with the keywords you entered.

You can also use a question mark at the end of a word as a 'wild card'. So searching for 'japan?' will return results like Japan, Japanese, Japanofile, Japanasaurus Rex, etc...
