As fellow language students, we wanted to create the best flash card program for studying words and phrases in Japanese & English.

JFlash has over 100 sets of bilingual data, an intelligent card selection algorithm that works with large & small sets, a dictionary searchable in English, Romaji or Japanese and a slick interface - all driven by a data engine!

This is only version 1.0, and we have a lot planned. We hope you enjoy the Japanese Flash!
