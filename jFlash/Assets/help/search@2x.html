Japanese Flash has well over 149,000 words separated into more than 180 sets. The search tool can return over 200 results in under 1 second using English, Romaji or Japanese keywords.

Search works best if you look for whole words and use the plain or infinitive form for verbs. You can also use multiple search terms at once in either language.

Try using a question mark at the end of a word as a wild card. So searching for japan? might return results like Japan, Japanese, Japanofile, Japanasaurus Rex, etc...
