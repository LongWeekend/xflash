Update Dictionary (30MB)

Thanks for Awesomely upgrading to Japanese Flash 1.1! Now that you have 1.1, you'll need to update your dictionary.

Please download & install the latest dictionary by pressing "Start" below.

You'll need a network connection during the download phase, and the entire update should take between 3-5 minutes (we recommend you use WiFi if your carrier charges you for bandwidth usage on 3G).

If now is not a convenient time, tap Cancel, and go to the Settings tab when you're ready.

You may want to know why you're downloading this update separately. That's a great question. Since you are Awesome (you purchased Japanese Flash after all!), we'll share Our Evil Master Plan.

We separated the dictionary, example sentences, and other components into "plug-ins" to make it easier to update the data as it changes.

For example, if someone finds an error in the dictionary, wouldn't it be great if it was fixed automatically? We are not there yet, but this release is a step in that direction.

So, here are the full details of the new features in Japanese Flash 1.1:

  • Inline Example Sentences

    While you are studying, swipe the card to see example sentences! You'll need to download the example sentences plug-in. Tap Settings, open the Plugins; section, and tap to download & install the example sentences after this update.

  • Custom Study Settings

    Some users told us that cards they know are shown too frequently. Other users told us that somewhat-known cards don't appear often enough. We make everyone happy by giving you control: tap "Settings" to adjust how Japanese Flash quizzes. You can set it to Easy, Medium, Hard, or choose your own custom settings.

  • Improved Dictionary Quality & Feedback Tool

    We've improved the quality of the dictionary (what you're downloading right now!). We've removed a few duplicate cards and fixed a few "word sense" errors. Best of all, we've turned the editorial hand over to you, the user. The Add To Set button on the Practice screen is now titled Action, and it does two things:

    Help Us Fix This Card: Find something wrong? Tell us - we'll incorporate the changes and push it back out to all Japanese Flash users.

    Add To Card Set: Allows you to add or remove the current card to and from different study sets (same behavior as 1.0).

  • Smaller Download Size

    By creating a plug-in structure, the base of Japanese Flash is now small enough to download "over the air" on a 3G network. Now, anyone can download Japanese Flash anywhere, anytime! Help us spread the word - the more people using JFlash, the stronger the dictionary will become!

  • Follow Us

    Okay, so it's not really a feature - but if you want to take a break from studying, check out the Follow Us section on the Settings tab. You can follow us on Twitter, or view Japanese Flash on Facebook. We update frequently with what we're doing - if you want to know what'll make it into the next version!
