Chinese Flash is more than just a flash card app; it's a tool to help you learn Chinese tone pronounciation, and to be able to differentiate tones.

We strongly recommend using a WiFi connection.

Here's how it works

After installation: Tap the "Audio" icon on each study card. You'll hear a pronounciation of the pinyin tones on that card, in order. Also, you can listen to music while studying -- your music will simply duck out while the tones are playing. Cool, right?

Each tone has been recorded by a native speaker; the tones are resources of Lingomi (http://lingomi.com/), used with permission under license.

Why do I have to download something?

We separated the components (dictionary search, audio, etc.) into downloadable updates to make it easier to deliver new data as it changes.

Enjoy the Awesome Pinyin Audio!
